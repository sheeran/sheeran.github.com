May 10th, 2013

This a Octopress blog entry sampler. All types of content that might find its way into a blog post, to include spiffy plug-in output such as code blocks, blockquotes, and images will be represented. While we’re at it: here is some emphasized text and might want to check strong text as well. Blog posts usually contain links, so here is an example.

A standard Markdown blockquote (George Orwell):

People sleep peaceably in their beds at night only because rough men stand ready to do violence on their behalf.
